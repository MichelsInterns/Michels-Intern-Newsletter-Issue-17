PROJECT SPOTLIGHT
Michels Construction team recently installed a permanent secant pile cutoff wall, a sturdy underground barrier that helps control water seepage and keeps the structure stable.

The team drilled 195 interlocking piles (each about a meter wide) more than 20 feet into limestone to make sure the structure stays solid.
